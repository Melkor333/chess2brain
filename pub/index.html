chess2brain

This is a small utility site to train the memorization of the chess board.

TLDR:

  • It will tell you a field on the chessboard
  • You try to figure out the color of the field (in your head)
  • After a short time it will tell the answer
  • It will wait another short time for you to visualize it some more
